Dermatologists Dr. Katie Rodan and Dr. Kathy Fields are the founders of Rodan + Fields. They met during their residencies at Stanford Medical School. We address your primary skin concern, such as signs of aging, dullness and discoloration, dryness and sensitivity or acne with an easy, step-by-step Regimen. Our Multi-Med Therapy uses the right ingredients in the right formulations in the right order to deliver real and visible results. More R+F products can be added for other concerns. Rodan + Fields uses groundbreaking technology so you can have the best-looking skin of your life.

ARE THE INGREDIENTS IN RODAN + FIELDS PRODUCTS SAFE?

The safety and efficacy of our products is our primary concern. We follow all FDA guidelines and constantly review ingredient information and testing data. We are members of the Personal Care Products Council (PCPC). We conduct irritancy testing on all of our products and our Regimens undergo dermatologist-run, third party clinical testing to ensure their safety and efficacy. For more information, please visit the PCPC website.

ARE YOUR PRODUCTS SAFE FOR PREGNANT OR NURSING WOMEN?

Every patient is different, so please always consult your doctor or health care professional prior to using our skincare products and tools and bring our Rodan + Fields Ingredient List along for more information.

DO YOU TEST PRODUCTS ON ANIMALS AND DO YOUR SUPPLIERS TEST PRODUCTS ON ANIMALS?

Rodan + Fields does not test its formulas on animals. We use alternate in-lab test methods, voluntary paid participants and often, home office staff volunteers to verify the safety and efficacy of our formulations.

WHAT IS THE SHELF LIFE OF YOUR PRODUCTS BEFORE AND AFTER OPENING?

Shelf life varies based on the specific formulation and type of product. The FDA requires that Over-the-Counter (OTC) products have an expiration date on inner (primary) and outer (secondary) packaging. Cosmetic (non-OTC) products that do not list an expiration date have a shelf life that is substantiated based on FDA shelf life testing guidelines. Unless specified on the packaging, the shelf life of cosmetic products is a minimum of two years.

IS PRODUCT PACKAGING RECYCLABLE? HOW CAN I TELL WHEN THERE ARE NO MARKINGS?

Most Rodan + Fields product containers are recyclable and all are manufactured and handled in accordance with all applicable federal, state and local environmental laws and regulations, including the strict California Hazardous Waste Act.

ARE RODAN + FIELDS PRODUCTS VEGAN?

Our products cannot be certified as vegan because some products contain natural or animal-derived ingredients, like milk or honey, in order to deliver our clinical results.

ARE RODAN + FIELDS PRODUCTS GLUTEN-FREE?

Almost all formulations contain no wheat or oat derivatives at this time. The only exceptions are SOOTHE Daily Mineral Sunscreen SPF 30 and SOOTHE Sensitive Skin Treatment which may contain or may have come in contact with gluten.

WHICH R+F PRODUCTS CONTAIN RETINOL?

Retinoids are forms of Vitamin A, and one of them, Retinol,is used in skincare products. Retinol has been favored by dermatologists for decades to encourage epidermal cell turnover to visibly improve skin texture and minimize the appearance of pores.
